CaloriesSnap Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us
Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Information We Collect

CaloriesSnap collects the following information, all of which is stored locally on your device:

  • Profile information you provide (name, age, weight, height, activity level)
  • Meal photos you capture or select
  • Nutritional analysis results (calories, macros, ingredients)
  • Daily calorie and macro goals

How We Use Your Information

  • Profile data is used to calculate your personalized daily calorie and macro goals
  • Meal photos are sent to a third-party AI service for nutritional analysis (see below)
  • Analysis results are stored on your device to track your daily nutrition

Third-Party AI Service

When you photograph or select a meal for analysis, the food image is sent to Groq (groq.com), a third-party AI infrastructure provider. Groq processes the image using Meta's Llama 4 Scout AI model to identify food items and estimate nutritional content.

What is sent to Groq:

  • The food photo only (compressed JPEG image)

What is NOT sent to Groq:

  • Your name, age, weight, height, or any profile information
  • Your meal history or past analysis results
  • Your device identifiers or location
  • Any other personal information

Photos are transmitted securely via HTTPS and are used solely for real-time food analysis. We do not control Groq's data retention practices; please refer to Groq's privacy policy for details on how they handle processed data.

Data Storage

All personal data, including your profile, meal logs, and meal photos, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No personal data is stored on our servers.

You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties except as described in the "Third-Party AI Service" section above. Food photos are shared with Groq solely for the purpose of nutritional analysis, and only after you grant explicit permission.

Your Consent

Before any food photo is sent to the AI service for analysis, the app presents a consent screen that clearly explains what data is sent and to whom. You must explicitly agree before any data is transmitted. You can decline, and no data will be sent to any external service.

Children's Privacy

CaloriesSnap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at calsnap.privacy@gmail.com.
